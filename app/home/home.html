Natural Resourcefulness

Our family is committed to the care of our animals and to an environment in which they thrive. We're focused on the optimal amount they can produce, not the maximum. We do our best to provide a stress free, healthy environment that keeps them happy, not just alive. That's because we have no doubt that happy animals produce better food.

We've been doing this for years now and it's amazing. Come by the farm on Saturdays from 10:00 to 2:00 to see how we do things. We have no secrets and we're proud of how we care for our animals and collect and package our food. We would love for you to see the difference it makes when you work with nature instead of against her.

We work hard every day to care for our animals and collect amazing food. We don't use chemicals in our farming and raise our animals on as much grass as possible. This means they have a lot more food that is exactly what they were born to eat and they love it. As a result, they produce some of the best tasting, vitamin rich, healthy, clean food you can get and we love it.

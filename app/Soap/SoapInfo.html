What are the benefits of Goat Milk Soap?

Goat milk contains skin nourishing Vitamin A, vitamin E, and vitamin C. It also contains some B vitamins, amino acids, and citric acids, mixed in with a bit of unsaturated fatty acids and zinc. I know, right? Makes you want to jump in a goat milk bath as soon as possible, but while that might not be possible, the next best thing is. Your skin likes these things and you could be satisfying those cravings every time you wash. All this mixed with natural creams in goat milk and proteins that kill acne-causing bacteria means good times for your sensitive skin.
